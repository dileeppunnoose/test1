Mount Carmel Church Kitchener

Welcome

Welcome to Mount Carmel Pentecostal Church – Kitchener – Malayalee Pentecostal Community

Our church is located in Kitchener, Ontario just minutes from the Fairview Mall, and with easy access to the major freeways. We have our Worship Service and Adult Bible study in Malayalam and our Sunday School for kids in English.

If you are in the Kitchener area and you are looking for a home church to attend or if you are just visiting the area, please come and worship with us. We look forward to worshiping with you, learning with you and from you, and serving the Lord.


Frequently Asked Questions

What time does Sunday Service begin?

Sunday service begins with Sunday School and Adult Bible Study at 10:00 AM. Our Worship Service begins at 10:30 AM.

Where are you located?

We are located in Kitchener, Ontario. 20 Rittenhouse Rd, Kitchener, Ontario N2C 1J3.

Is there anything more than just Sunday?

Of course! We have Fasting prayer meetings every first Friday of the month, Youth meetings bi-monthly and occasional Saturday meetings with guest pastors. Be sure to check the calendar for upcoming events.
